F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Is computer fluency a requirement for joining the club?

While not a requirement, prior experience with computers is preferred. Nevertheless, even if you don't possess strong computer skills, joining the club offers valuable learning opportunities.

Do I need any knowledge on programming?

No, programming skills are not required to become a basic member. However, possessing some computer-related skills is necessary for official membership, which can be acquired through our comprehensive learning resources.

What do your learning resources contain?

Everything from programming to designing is available to all members.

What skills are preferred for joining this club?

Proficiency in any computer-related skill is preferred, ranging from programming and modeling to music composition and robotics

How do I join the community server?

Officially become a member and request the server link from one of the board members.

IMPORTANT extra information

If you possess advanced fluency in computers and programming, you are welcome to join the club at no cost.
